Travel

How to get to the venue.

By train: Durham Station is the closest stop, with regular services from London, York, Newcastle and beyond.

By air: Newcastle and Durham Tees Valley are the nearest airports. From there, trains or taxis will bring you into Durham. It is however entirely manageable to fly into London and take the train up.

By car: Durham is just off the A1(M). Please check local parking options ahead of time, we have provided parking recommendations in the FAQ.

Where to stay

Some recommended Options

We have Castle accommodation reserved for the night before and the night of, bookable via codes.

  • These codes will be reserved to international guests and then on a first-come first-served basis.
  • On the RSVP form, please request if you would like to stay on site and we'll distribute the codes as soon as possible.

Hotels & B&Bs in central Durham (short walk / taxi to the castle):

  • Hotel Indigo : a good hotel option, slightly more expensive than the Marriott, newly renovated with good rooms.
  • Radisson Blu : Not my style, but a good option if you want to also do a business conference that weekend.
  • Delta Hotel Marriott : A great option for anyone, especially those with Marriot points.
  • Premier Inn & B&Bs in central Durham (short walk / taxi to the castle):

Airbnbs / apartments may be a good option if you're coming as a group or staying longer.

We recommend booking early – Durham is small and popular, so rooms can disappear quickly.
