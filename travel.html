Travel

Your quest to Durham Castle, simplified.

By train: Durham Station is the closest stop, with regular services from London, York, Newcastle and beyond.

By air: Newcastle and Durham Tees Valley are the nearest airports. From there, trains or taxis will bring you into Durham.

By car: Durham is just off the A1(M). Please check local parking options ahead of time, we have provided parking recommendations in the FAQ.

Where to stay

Some options to get you started (feel free to choose your own adventure).

  • We have limited spaces available inside the Castle for guests to stay. These spots will be reserved for international guests and close family, but please request if you would like to stay on site and we'll see what we can do!
  • Hotels & B&Bs in central Durham (short walk / taxi to the castle):
    • Hotel Indigo : a good hotel option, newly renovated with good rooms
    • Radisson Blu : Not my style but a good option if you want to also do a business conference in between.
