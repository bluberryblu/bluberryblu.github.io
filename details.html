The Day

A summary of what to expect.

Date: Saturday, 5 September 2026

Location: Durham Castle, Durham, UK

The ceremony, drinks, dinner and dancing will all be within the castle and grounds, so once you arrive the castle is ours for the day to explore.

Schedule (approx)

12:00 pm
Those staying at the castle arrive, check in & get settled.
1:00 pm
Service guests arrive & get settled in Tunstall Chapel.
1:30 pm
Service in Tunstall Chapel.
2:30 pm
Drinks, Canapes & photos in the fellows garden (sunny) or in the castle (rainy).
Other guests arrive.
4:00 pm
Dinner & speeches in the Great Hall
7:30 pm
First dance & party time!
12:00 pm
Wrap up the night
Next day (8:00am - 10:00am)
Breakfast in the Castle Great Hall for those staying in the castle, and time for anyone see the bride and groom and say final goodbyes.

Dress code: Festive Formal - Don't be afraid to upstage the Bride & Groom!

We hope this celebration feels joyful, playful, romantic and fun.

We invite guests to reflect that spirt through festive attire - stylish, expressive and celebratory.

Think bright colours, bold textures or prints, interesting silhouettes, or statement pieces.

This is a chance to have fun with your outfit and embrace something a little more playful or glamorous than usual.

That said, we want everyone to feel comfortable and there is absolutely no pressure to go all out. Please wear whatever makes you feel confident and at ease while still leaning into the festive spirit.

  • Festive Wedding Attire

Weather: September in Durham can get cold - expect a feel between 5°C (~40°F) and 20°C (~70°F). Bring layers, a coat, which we hope you won't need until the evening!
