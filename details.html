The Day

All the where / when / what-on-earth-do-I-do info.

Date: Saturday, 5 September 2026

Location: Durham Castle, Durham, UK

The ceremony, drinks, dinner and dancing will all be within the castle and grounds, so once you arrive the castle is ours for the day to explore.

Schedule (approx)

12:00 pm

1:00 pm
Service guests arrive & get settled in Tunstall Chapel.

Service in Tunstall Chapel.
2:30 pm
Drinks, Canapes & photos in the fellows garden (sunny) or in the castle (rainy).
Other guests arrive.
4:00 pm

7:30 pm
First dance & party time!
12:00 pm
Carriages / sleepy hugs

Dress code: Festive Formal - Don't be afraid to upstage the Bride & Groom!

Think: a balance between formal and playful, encouraging the use of holiday colors, festive fabrics like velvet and sequins, and bold accessories while maintaining a polished look. For men, this means suits or blazers with a festive tie, pocket square, or colorful shoes, while women can opt for dresses, jumpsuits, or elevated separates like a silk top with tailored pants or a statement skirt. The key is to be creative and incorporate seasonal flair in a way that is still respectful of the occasion.

Weather: September in Durham can get cold - expect a feel between 5°C (~40°F) and 20°C (~70°F). Bring layers, a coat, which we hope you won't need until the evening!
