FAQ

If you’re wondering it, someone else probably is too.

Can I bring a plus one? +

Our venue requires numbers to be known in advance, so we can only accommodate the guests named on the email invitations sent out. If you're unsure, please ask us!

Are children invited? +

Edit this answer to match your plans – e.g. “We love all children immensely, however similar to the plus one policy, we can only accommodate the guests named on the email invitations sent out.” If you're unsure, please ask us!

What time should we arrive? +

This will depend on what capacity you are attending the wedding: If you've been invited to the ceremony, please aim to arrive by 1:00 pm to get seated before the ceremony at 1:30 pm. If you're attending the wedding breakfast, please aim to arrive between 2pm - 3:30 pm to get seated before the meal at 4:00 pm.

Is there parking at the venue? +

Durham Castle has limited parking available for guests staying overnight. For day guests, we recommend using public transport or nearby parking facilities in Durham city centre: A full listing of all Durham car parks Prince Bishops Car Park, City, Durham DH1 3UJ - the closest car park, but limited spaces. The Sands Multi-storey, Riverside Place, Durhan, DH1 1SL - a slightly longer walk, but larger capacity.

I have dietary restrictions – what should I do? +

Please note any dietary requirements when you RSVP, and we'll make sure you're well looked after. If these dietary restrictions change, please let us know and we can try to accomodate!
